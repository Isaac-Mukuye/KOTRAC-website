# KOTRAC Digital Orthopaedic Rehabilitation Network

Kakira Orthopaedic and Trauma Centre (KOTRAC) is a health innovation initiative focused on expanding access to orthopaedic and rehabilitation services for underserved rural communities in Uganda.

This repository contains the prototype website and initial concept for the **KOTRAC Digital Orthopaedic Rehabilitation Platform**, a tele-rehabilitation system designed to connect rural trauma patients with orthopaedic specialists and physiotherapists through digital technology.

## Problem

Many patients in rural Africa recovering from fractures, spinal injuries, or childhood disabilities do not complete rehabilitation due to:

- Long distances to hospitals
- Limited number of physiotherapists and orthopaedic specialists
- High travel and treatment costs

This often results in long-term disability and reduced quality of life.

## Solution

KOTRAC is developing a **digital rehabilitation network** that combines telemedicine with community-based care.

Key components include:

- Tele-rehabilitation consultations with orthopaedic specialists
- Digital physiotherapy exercise programs
- Video-based progress monitoring
- Community Rehabilitation Assistants (CRAs) who support patients locally
- Referral system for patients requiring in-person care

## Platform Features

- Patient registration and injury tracking
- Digital physiotherapy exercise library
- Video upload for rehabilitation progress
- Specialist feedback and monitoring
- Community rehabilitation assistant support

## Impact Vision

The long-term goal is to build a scalable digital rehabilitation system that:

- Improves recovery outcomes after trauma
- Reduces disability in rural communities
- Expands access to orthopaedic care
- Supports thousands of patients across underserved regions of Africa

## Location

Kakira, Jinja District  
Uganda

## Status

Early-stage prototype under development. The platform is being designed as part of a broader effort to expand digital health access and community-based rehabilitation services.
