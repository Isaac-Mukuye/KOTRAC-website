Kakira Orthopaedic and Trauma Centre (KOTRAC)

Expanding access to Orthopaedic and physiotherapy care digitally

About KOTRAC

KOTRAC is a health innovation initiative working to expand access to orthopaedic and rehabilitation services for rural communities in Uganda. The project combines digital health tools, tele-rehabilitation, and trained community rehabilitation assistants to connect patients with specialists and improve recovery outcomes after injury or disability.

Our Solution

Tele-Rehabilitation

Remote physiotherapy sessions connecting rural patients with specialists.

Exercise Library

Digital rehabilitation programs for fractures, spinal injuries, and disability care.

Community Rehab Assistants

Locally trained assistants supporting patients with exercises and monitoring progress.

Specialist Feedback

Patients upload progress videos and receive guidance from orthopaedic professionals.

Impact Vision

Our goal is to build a scalable digital orthopaedic rehabilitation network that improves recovery outcomes, reduces disability, and expands access to specialist care for underserved rural populations across Africa.
